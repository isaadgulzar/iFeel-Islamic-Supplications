Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save duas for later?

Tap the heart icon on any dua to add it to your favorites. Access your saved duas from the Favorites tab.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All duas are stored locally on your device and work completely offline.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings and select your preferred language for translations.

Why don't I see analytics consent?

The consent modal appears on first launch. If you've already made a choice, you can change it in Settings (coming in future update).

Is my data private?

Yes! Your favorites and preferences stay on your device. Optional analytics are completely anonymous. See our Privacy Policy for details.
