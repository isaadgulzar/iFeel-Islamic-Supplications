Introduction

iFeel: Duas for Every Feeling ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Favorites & Preferences: When you favorite duas or adjust display settings, this information is stored locally on your device only.
  • Language Settings: Your preferred language and display options are saved on your device.

Analytics Information (Optional)

If you consent to analytics, we collect:

  • Usage Data: Which duas you read, which categories you view, search queries
  • App Interactions: App opens, navigation patterns, feature usage
  • Device Information: Device type (iOS/Android), app version
  • Anonymous Identifiers: A randomly generated device ID (not linked to you personally)
What We Do NOT Collect:
  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ IP addresses
  • ❌ Precise location data
  • ❌ Device identifiers (IDFA, Android Advertising ID)
  • ❌ Contacts, photos, or other sensitive data
  • ❌ Information about other apps on your device

How We Use Information

Local Data (Stored on Your Device Only)

  • Favorites: To show your saved duas across app sessions
  • Preferences: To remember your language and display settings
  • Reading History: To track milestones (10 duas read, etc.)

This data never leaves your device.

Analytics Data (If You Consent)

  • Improve the App: Understand which duas are most helpful
  • Fix Bugs: Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Add Features: Develop new features based on usage patterns
  • Serve the Community: See which duas help most Muslims

Analytics are powered by PostHog (posthog.com), a privacy-focused analytics platform.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except:

  • Analytics Provider: PostHog processes anonymous usage data on our behalf (only if you consent)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation

Your Choices

Analytics Consent

  • Opt-In: You can enable analytics to help us improve the app
  • Opt-Out: You can decline or disable analytics anytime
  • Change Anytime: Future updates will allow changing your choice in Settings

Your Rights

  • Access: View what data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app deletes all local data
  • Decline: You can use the full app without enabling analytics

Data Security

  • Local Storage: Your favorites and preferences are encrypted using industry-standard device encryption
  • Analytics Transmission: If enabled, data is sent over HTTPS (encrypted connection)
  • No Accounts: We don't require accounts, so there's no password to compromise
  • Minimal Data: We only collect what's necessary to improve the app

Children's Privacy

iFeel is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from anyone, this app is safe for children to use.

Third-Party Services

PostHog Analytics (Optional)

If you enable analytics, we use PostHog for anonymous usage tracking. PostHog's privacy policy: posthog.com/privacy

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts (Inter, Amiri) loaded locally in the app. No data is sent to Google.

Expo

Built with Expo (expo.dev). Expo may collect crash reports to improve the framework. Expo's privacy policy: expo.dev/privacy

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 1 year in PostHog (free tier), then automatically deleted
  • No Personal Data: Since we don't collect personal information, there's nothing to delete

International Users

iFeel is available worldwide. Analytics data (if enabled) is processed in the United States by PostHog. By using analytics, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing an in-app notification
  • Requesting re-consent if data collection practices change

Islamic Values & Ethics

As an Islamic app, we are committed to:

  • Amanah (Trustworthiness): Being transparent about data practices
  • Privacy (Sitr): Respecting your privacy as a fundamental right
  • Community Benefit: Using data only to serve the Muslim ummah better
  • No Harm: Never selling data or using it for advertising
